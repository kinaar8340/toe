# Contributing to Aaron’s Theory of Everything

Thank you for your interest in contributing to **Aaron’s Theory of Everything** (Flux Flywheels, Gauged Hopf Lattice, and Emergent Reality)!

This is an open, speculative physics project focused on reproducibility, clarity, and scientific honesty. We welcome contributions from researchers, developers, physicists, mathematicians, and curious minds who want to help refine, test, and explore the model.

## Ways to Contribute

You can contribute in many ways:

- **Code & Reproduction** — Improve scripts, add tests, or enhance the one-click reproduction pipeline.
- **Documentation & Papers** — Fix typos, improve LaTeX clarity, or add new sections to the papers.
- **Ideas & Theory** — Propose refinements to observer synchronization, residual leakage bounds, propulsion concepts, or new falsifiable predictions.
- **Bug Reports & Issues** — Report problems with reproduction, invariants, or installation.
- **Discussions** — Join theoretical or implementation discussions in the GitHub Discussions tab.
